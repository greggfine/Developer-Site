GREGG FINE

Full Stack Web Developer

Hello, my name is Gregg Fine. I’m a New Jersey/NYC-based Full Stack web developer specializing mostly in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and Node. I live in Westfield, NJ with my wife Erica, my daughter Eva, and my dogs Penny and Maya.

After several years as a producer of music for the world’s leading brands, I discovered a true passion for web development and programming. I've mastered my software engineering capacities over the past two years through rigorous self-study.

I hold a dual-degree from the New School for Social Research: a Bachelor of Fine Arts from The Mannes College of Music and a Bachelor of Arts from Eugene Lang College.

I’m also a published educator with 17 courses(to date) on topics ranging from Music Theory, Jazz Improvisation, Ear Training, to Guitar Effects and Technology.

My experience as an educator provides me with excellent written and verbal communication skills along with exceptional problem-solving skills. My passion is in learning new technologies, self-improvement, and efficiency.
